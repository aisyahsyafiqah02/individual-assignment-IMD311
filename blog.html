My stories

I will never be able to describe in words what my family means to me. If anyone wants to know, they will have to go inside my heart to see. I really appreciate the way my family members have come out from a difficult situation and feel lucky to be a part of this superb and loving family. Families and friends form some of the closest bonds you will ever experience. It is almost impossible for you to lead a happy life without support from these important groups of people.

  • My first brother work as the engineering in Puspakom, married at the age of 29 years old and have 2 children
  • My second brother work as medical assistant in a hospital at Negeri Sembilan, married at the age of 27 years old and have 1 children
  • My third brother work as chef assistant in a big restaurant at Kuala Terengganu, married at the age of 25 years old
  • My fourth brother work as a office clerk in a land office in Kuala Terengganu

Lastly, is about my parent. My father was already gone when I was still a baby and my mother is the one who support us until we grow up. my mother is a strong woman. she is a hardworking woman and love her children equally. since I'am the only girl in my family I need to work even harder to make my mother proud. A mother’s love is unpredictable. A mother starts loving her baby even before the birth of her child. A mother’s love is a selfless love that does not expect anything in return.

My Friends

We all have different friends who brighten up our lives in their own different ways. However their characteristics might be unbearable, each of them is an inseparable part of our lives. Friendship is a bond that cannot be broken by time or distance. In reality, it’s not the distance that makes friends grow apart, it’s the lack of effort and communication. Sometimes I think, ‘What is a friend?’ Then I say, ‘Friend is someone to share the last cookie with.

My Grandmother

A grandmother in one’s life is the person who works for her family even if she has to sacrifice her own dreams just for the betterment of her family. She is that one person who is going to work for her whole life for the well-being of her loved ones, to make their life as comfortable as possible.

My cat

Perhaps one reason we are fascinated by cats is because such a small animal can contain so much independence, dignity, and freedom of spirit. Unlike the dog, the cat’s personality is never bet on a human’s. He demands acceptance on his own terms
